## Personalization Variables

| Variable | Source |
|---|---|
| `[First Name]` | Owner name (manual research or LinkedIn) |
| `[Company Name]` | CSV column: Company Name |
| `[City]` | CSV column: City |
| `[Profile URL]` | CSV column: Profile URL |

## Send Tips

- Send Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am local time
- Use a personal Gmail/gsuite address — not a bulk sender — for deliverability
- Limit to 30–40 sends/day to avoid spam flags
- Personalize at minimum: company name, city, profile URL
- Track opens with a tool like Streak or Mailtrack
